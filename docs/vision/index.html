Our Vision

The Skip The Service app will be used to allow homeowners to both access any service as well as provide services to others in one convenient location. Like the popular food delivery apps, SkipTheService will allow users to view selective service categories and read user reviews to find and book any service from beauty and wellness for men and women to home repairs and maintenance.
